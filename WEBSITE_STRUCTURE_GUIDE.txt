PART A - SWIPER LOOP WARNING (SHORT EXPLANATION)
1. Why warning appears:
- Swiper loop needs enough slides for the current slidesPerView/slidesPerGroup values.
- If not enough slides exist, loop becomes unstable and warning is shown.
2. Is it mandatory to fix:
- Not always a blocker, but recommended for production-quality UX/log cleanliness.
3. Practical fixes:
- Add/duplicate slides, or reduce slidesPerView, or disable loop for that breakpoint.

- Imports from /images/... inside components.
